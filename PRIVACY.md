# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 11, 2026_

PhotoScore is a small project that grades the product photos on a Shopify store. This page explains, plainly, what it collects and what it does with it.

## What we collect

- **The store URL you enter.** Used to fetch your public product images and grade them.
- **Your email address**, only if you choose to submit it to receive the full report.
- **Basic usage events** (page views, when an audit runs, when a link is clicked) and the `utm_source` of the link you arrived from, so we can see which channels work.

We do not collect payment details, passwords, or any data from inside your Shopify admin. We only read publicly visible product images.

## How we use it

- To generate your Photo Score and per-product report.
- To email you the full report, if you asked for it.
- To understand, in aggregate, which distribution channels bring people in.

We do **not** sell your data or share it with advertisers.

## Where it lives

Data is stored on our own server as simple log files. Your email is used to send you the report and is not added to any third-party mailing list without your action.

## Your choices

Want your email or store data removed? Email **psm.pablosanchez@gmail.com** and it will be deleted.
